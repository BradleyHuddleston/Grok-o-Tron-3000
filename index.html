Human IQ tests lean on stuff like spatial puzzles or vocab—cool for us janky humans, but lame for AIs. The Grok-o-Tron 3000 hits what AIs rock at: lightning-fast logic, vast knowledge, creative twists, and code-smarts. No paper folding here—just pure digital brainpower!
